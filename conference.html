ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The conference will provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and results in theory, methodology and applications impacts and challenges of sustainable developments in Civil Engineering globally.

OBJECTIVES OF THE CONFERENCE

The main focus of the conference is to disseminate useful information about the recent trends in Civil Engineering domain for sustainable development. The main objective of the conference is to bring together leading academicians technologists, researchers, PG students and research scholars, to exchange and share their experiences and research outcomes on all aspects of construction materials and technologies for sustainable development in Civil Engineering. The new developments in Civil Engineering one expected to provide better solutions for infrastructure design and development with a strong emphasis on sustainability. In view of changing technological needs of civil engineering, all over the globe, an exponential growth has been observed in terms of R&D in the various domain such as structural engineering, composite structures, materials etc. The conference is expected to address well, all such recent and state of the art R&D issues.
